About Me

Hello there. I’m Gabriel, a 36 year old guy from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I’m a Business Intelligence Analyst, freelance web developer, IT Engineering undergrad and future husband.

I have always dreamed of making a game and developing it as a business and now I’m giving it a real shot. I’d like to make it my living and if not, at least I’ll learn a lot of new things to beef up my resume.

Made this blog to cheer me up in the process and discuss some ideas with those who may have the slightest interest in a game like this. Life can give but also take. I’ll do my best to have this project become a “give” with my will, hard work and your support!

For non IM related things, you may reach me @GabrielZorrilla. Thanks for stopping by!
